About

Reputation Precedes You

PilotRep is a third-party reputation platform built for the pilots, corporations and alliances of New Eden. In a universe where trust is a currency and betrayal is a profession knowing who you're flying with matters.

We give the EVE Online community a neutral structured space to record and review their encounters. From reliable logistics runners to known scammers, war criminals to stalwart allies. Every rep submitted is a data point in a living record of New Eden's social fabric.

PilotRep is not affiliated with Fenris Creations. We are an independent fan project operating under the EVE Online Third-Party Developer license. All EVE-related assets remain the property of Fenris Creations.
